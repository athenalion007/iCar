Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How accurate is the AI detection?

A: iCar uses advanced CoreML models for local AI analysis. Paint detection accuracy is approximately 85-90%, tire depth detection error is within ±0.5mm, and engine sound diagnosis is based on spectral analysis technology. Detection results are for reference only; for major faults, please consult a professional technician.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Absolutely secure. All data (photos, recordings, inspection reports) is processed locally on your device and will not be uploaded to any server. You can delete all data at any time through the app settings.

Q: Why does the app need so many permissions?

A: Each permission corresponds to a core function: Camera for capturing vehicle photos, Microphone for recording engine sounds, Location for recording inspection locations, and Motion sensors for suspension vibration analysis. All permissions can be managed in system settings at any time.

Q: Which vehicle models are supported?

A: iCar supports all brands and models of cars, SUVs, and light trucks. The AI models have been trained on extensive vehicle data and can identify most common vehicle issues.

Q: How do I export inspection reports?

A: On the report details page, tap the "Share Report" button to export a complete inspection report in PDF format, which can be shared with repair technicians via email, messaging apps, etc.

Feature Usage Guides

Tire Inspection

1
Enter the "Tire Inspection" feature and select the tire position to inspect (front left, front right, rear left, rear right)
2
Point the camera at the tire tread, ensuring adequate lighting and clear visibility of the tire surface
3
Tap the capture button, and AI will automatically analyze tread depth and wear patterns
4
View the inspection report to understand tire health status and recommended actions

Engine Diagnosis

1
Enter the "Engine Diagnosis" feature and ensure the vehicle is in idle state
2
Hold your phone's microphone near the engine compartment (approximately 30-50cm away)
3
Tap the record button and record 10-15 seconds of engine sound
4
AI will analyze spectral characteristics to identify potential fault types

Troubleshooting

App Cannot Access Camera

  • Go to iOS Settings > iCar > Camera and ensure permission is enabled
  • Check if another app is currently using the camera
  • Restart the app or device

AI Detection Results Are Inaccurate

  • Ensure the shooting environment has adequate lighting, avoiding shadows and reflections
  • Keep the camera steady to avoid blur
  • Make sure the area being inspected is within the frame
  • Clean the camera lens

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Ensure iOS version is 16.0 or higher
  • Close other background apps to free up memory
  • Check if device storage is sufficient (recommend keeping 2GB+ free)
  • Try restarting the device
