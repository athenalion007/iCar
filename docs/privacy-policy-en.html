iCar Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026 | Effective Date: April 27, 2026

Important Notice: iCar ("we", "this app") highly values your privacy protection. This privacy policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using this app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide vehicle AI detection services, we collect the following types of information:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Vehicle Photos: Photos used for AI paint damage detection and tire tread depth analysis
  • Engine Recordings: Engine sound recordings used for AI fault diagnosis

1.2 Device Information

  • Device Identification: Device model, operating system version (for app functionality optimization only)
  • Camera and Microphone: For capturing vehicle photos and recording engine sounds

2. How We Use Your Information

Information Type Purpose Processing Method
Vehicle Photos AI paint damage detection, tire tread depth analysis Local processing, no server upload
Engine Recordings AI sound fault diagnosis Real-time local analysis, recordings not saved

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Data Storage Location

  • All data is stored locally on your device only, using iOS CoreData and file system
  • Inspection reports and photos are stored in the app's sandbox directory
  • We do not upload any of your data to cloud servers

3.2 Data Security Measures

  • App data is protected by iOS sandbox mechanism, inaccessible to other apps
  • Uses iOS standard encryption APIs to protect sensitive data
  • Supports Face ID/Touch ID protection for app access (if enabled by user)

4. Data Retention & Deletion

4.1 Data Retention Period

  • Inspection Reports: Retained permanently until you delete them
  • Vehicle Photos: Retained with associated inspection reports
  • Recording Data: Deleted immediately after analysis, not retained
  • Sensor Data: Processed in real-time, not saved

4.2 Your Right to Delete

You can delete data through the following methods:

  • Delete individual inspection reports within the app
  • Clear all data in app settings
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all app data

5. Third-Party Sharing

Important Statement: We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information with any third parties. All data processing is completed locally on your device.

6. Children's Privacy Protection (COPPA Compliance)

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately to delete it.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: View the data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit certain data processing activities
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data

8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified to you through in-app notifications. Continued use of this app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
